# Privacy Policy for Break & Run

**Last updated:** December 2, 2025

## Introduction

Break & Run ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

**Break & Run does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information.**

### Local Data Storage

The app stores all data locally on your device using Android's DataStore system. This includes:
- Game scores and statistics
- Player names (as entered by you)
- Game history and match records
- Active game state

**This data never leaves your device** and is not transmitted to any servers or third parties.

## Data We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect:
- Personal identification information
- Location data
- Device identifiers
- Usage analytics
- Crash reports (unless you explicitly enable them through Android system settings)
- Any data that could identify you personally

## Third-Party Services

Break & Run does not use any third-party services, analytics tools, advertising networks, or data collection services.

## Internet Connection

Break & Run does not require an internet connection to function. All features work completely offline.

## Data Storage

All app data is stored locally on your device. If you uninstall the app, all data will be deleted from your device.

## Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device:
- You have complete control over your data
- You can delete all data by uninstalling the app
- No data is shared with us or any third parties

## Children's Privacy

Break & Run does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. Since we do not collect any data, this is not applicable.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.

## Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the app's support channels or GitHub repository.

## Consent

By using Break & Run, you consent to this Privacy Policy. Since we do not collect any data, your use of the app is your consent to store data locally on your device only.
